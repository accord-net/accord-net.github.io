Remarks

One of the most prominent features on this namespace is the great number of probability distributions , kernel functions, and hypothesis tests available to build your statistics processing applications. A great number of statistical analysis, including classical and kernel-based ones are also available to help analyze, process and simplify your data.

This namespace is also home for the one of the most used features of the framework, the Hidden Markov models and Conditional Random Fields . Such models can be used to build sequence classifiers, perform gesture recognition, and can even be combined with neural networks to create hybrid models uniting those two technologies. Other models include regression and survival models.
