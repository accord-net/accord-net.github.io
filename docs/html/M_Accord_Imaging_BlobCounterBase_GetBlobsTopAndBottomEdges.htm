Remarks

The method scans each column of the blob and finds the most top and the most bottom points for it adding them to appropriate lists. The method may be very useful in conjunction with different routines from Accord.Math.Geometry, which allow finding convex hull or quadrilateral's corners.

 Note
Both lists of points are sorted by X coordinate - points with smaller X value go first.
