Remarks

The Accord.Vision namespace houses the popular Haar Cascade Classifier, also known as the Viola-Jones object detector. This namespace also offers diverse cascade definitions which can be used to track different parts of the human body. Custom cascades can also be loaded from OpenCV-compatible XML cascade definition files, and should work seamlessly even when containing tilted cascade features, or multi-node (tree nodes) cascade definitions.

The namespace also offers tracking algorithms, such as Bradski's Camshift, the much simpler HSL blob tracker algorithm and even a template matching tracker.
